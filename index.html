A Student on a Mission

👨‍🔬

Hi! I'm Lorenzo, an IGCSE student who is absolutely obsessed with biology. From the moment I learned about DNA replication to discovering how fungi can control ant brains, I knew this was my calling.

But here's what drives me even more: not everyone has the opportunity to learn. Many brilliant students around the world don't have access to quality biology education. That's why I created BioSphere—to share my passion and help underprivileged students who deserve the same chance to fall in love with life science.

I believe biology shouldn't be boring memorization. It should be about wonder, curiosity, and discovery. Whether you're preparing for your IGCSE exams or just curious about how life works, I'm here to help—completely free.
